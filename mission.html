Our Mission

Standish Methodist Church exists to worship God, our Heavenly Farther and to make disciples of Jesus Christ through the power of the Holy Spirit, by being:

Open to God

"I am the way, the truth and the light"

John 14 Verse 6

Open to Life


"For God so loved the world that he gave his only Son, that everyone who believes in Him will not perish but have eternal life"

John 3 Verse 16

Open to You


"Just as I have loved you, you should love each other. Your love for one another will prove to the world that you are my disciples"

John 13 Verse 34
